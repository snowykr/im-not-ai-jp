## Contributing

Reports are most useful when they include:

- the Japanese text sample or a short excerpt
- the model or tool that generated the draft, if known
- the genre, such as business email, public notice, report, blog, or UI text
- the pattern you noticed, such as register monotony, translationese, repeated
  connectors, honorific mismatch, dense noun chains, or over-structured bullets

Contributions are accepted under the same MIT license.
